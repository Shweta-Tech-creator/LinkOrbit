Why We Built LinkOrbit?

Managing multiple social media platforms was exhausting — endless tab switching, missed updates, and scattered insights made it feel like chaos. We created LinkOrbit to change that: a single orbit where everything connects seamlessly. Our mission is simple — give you back time, focus, and control.
